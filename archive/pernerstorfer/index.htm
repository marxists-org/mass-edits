Marxist Writers: Engelbert Pernerstorfer

Engelbert Pernerstorfer Archive

1850 – 1918

'Socialism will be the first to create a condition consciously, in which no one will be prevented by restrictive social forms from becoming a whole man.'

Englebert Pernerstorfer (1911), 'Theatre and Democracy', Der Strom, 390.

The bibliography below is offered as a guide to the scope of Pernerstorfer's contributions. Links will appear as articles are added.
