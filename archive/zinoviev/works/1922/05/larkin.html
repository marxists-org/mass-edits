G. Zinoviev


In the International

Telegram of the Communist International
to Jim Larkin

(27 May 1922)

Source: International Press Correspondence, Vol. 2 No. 44, 2 June 1922, pp. 334.
On-line Publication: Zinoviev Internet Archive, December 2019.
Transcription/Mark-up: Einde O’Callaghan for the Marxists’ Internet Archive.

To Jim Larkin: Moscow, May 27, 1922

The Communist International sends its warmest greetings to the undaunted fighter released from the “democratic” prisons.

Zinoviev
