Tony Cliff

Russia: A Marxist Analysis

(1964)

This text is an extended version of Stalinist Russia: A Marxist Analysis, which was originally published in 1955.
This edition was first published in 1964 and reprinted in 1970.
The book State Capitalism in Russia, published in 1974, consists of Chapters 1–9 and Chapter 18 (slightly edited) of this edition.
A number of obvious typesetting errors have been corrected without comment.
Transcribed & marked up by Dylan Stillwood, Rolf Vorhaug & Einde O’Callaghan for the Marxists’ Internet Archive.
