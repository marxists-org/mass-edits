We regret to announce the death of Comrade Mrs. Dora Montefiore. She was an old Socialist and a member of the Communist Party at its formation.

Ill-health and loss of sight prevented her from taking an active part in the movement in recent years.

Mrs. Montefiore was an old and close friend of Clara Zetkin.

The funeral is to take place at Golders Green to-day at 12.30 p.m.
