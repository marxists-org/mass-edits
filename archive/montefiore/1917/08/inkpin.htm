Letter addressed:— Mr Albert Inkpin,
81A Maiden Lane
Strand. London. W.C.

Postmark illegible.

 *  *  *  *  *  *  *  

Rocks Hall,
Crowborough.
Sussex.

Dear Comrade Inkpin,
I would much like to go as a Delegate to the Allied Socialist Congress on Tuesday where I see you are to have 8 representatives. I don't know if you have anyone to translate speeches from the French but if not I shall be very glad to be of use.

Yours sincerely,
(sd) J.B. Montefiore.
