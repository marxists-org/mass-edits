Yetta Barsh Shachtman

1915 – 1996

A member of the socialist movement in the United States for most of her life. Member of the Communist League of America, and along with her husband, Max, Socialists Workers Party, WP, ISL and finally the SD, USA. She was heavily involved with issues surrounding education. For corrections, comments and additions contact Einde O’Callaghan.
