K. Radek & B. Kohn

Radio

(7 June 1922)

From International Press Correspondence, Vol. 2 No. 46, 9 June 1922, pp. 344.
Transcribed & marked up by Einde O’Callaghan for the Marxists’ Internet Archive.

The following radio relating to the defenders of the Social Revolutionaries has been received from Moscow:

In the trial of the S.R.’s, Zetkin, Muna and Bokanyi will represent the E.C. of the Comintern; Sadoul and Kohn [1] will represent those former S.R.’s now supporting the Soviet Power; Frossard, Smeral, Sadoul, Bell and Jordanoff will act as experts.

Moscow, June 7th

Radek, Kohn

* * *

Note by MIA

1. Kohn was a pseudonym of the Hungarian Communist leader Béla Kun.
