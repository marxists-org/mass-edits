Prefatory Note

It is now 20 years since the Fourth International was founded.

In order briefly to make known its history, its policy, and its present goals, we think it useful to publish the text of a lecture I gave on this subject at the Summer School of the independent Labour Party, at Ruskin College, Oxford, on August 12th 1958.

Michel Pablo
