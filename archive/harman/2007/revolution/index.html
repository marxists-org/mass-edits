About the author

Chris Harman is the author of A People’s History of the World (Bookmarks 1998), Economics of the Madhouse (Bookmarks 1995) and The Lost Revolution: Germany 1918 to 1923 (Bookmarks 1982). He is the editor of International Socialism and is a leading member of the Socialist Workers Party (www.swp.org.uk).
