Paul Foot

Stop the Cuts

What to Fight For:

A Programme for Struggle

 
1. No redundancies! No Cuts!

2. Insist on Full Establishment. No Unfilled Vacancies.

3. No Substitution by Voluntary Services or by Contract Agency Labour.

4. No co-operation with Rationalisation or Participation.

5. For strong rank and file trade union organisation:–

  • 100 per cent trade union closed shops.
  • Trade union meetings during working hours.
  • Shop stewards or representatives elected from every section.
  • Joint shop stewards committees, white collar and manual, in every public sector establishment.
