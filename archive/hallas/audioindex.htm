Duncan Hallas

This is a small audio archive of talks given by Duncan Hallas at Marxism, an event organised by the British SWP every year in London at the beginning of July. We would like to thank the ResistanceMP3 Website for allowing us to copy these talks.

As many of these talks were recorded by individuals using a hand-held cassette recorder the quality is not always optimal. The sound quality can often be improved by adjusting the volume, bass and treble controls or by listening on headphones.
